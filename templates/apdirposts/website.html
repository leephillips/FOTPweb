About This Website

This is the place to find out about Planetarium shows and special events and to buy tickets. Many of our shows become sold out, so reserve your spot early!

These and other important announcements are listed under “Events & Shows” or “Main Articles.” Other content is listed under “Science,” where you can find articles about astronomy and related subjects, as well as an astronomy newsfeed, updated approximately daily, and “Directors’ Corner,” a place for special articles by members of our board. Please visit the “Join, Donate, Subscribe” link to support the Planetarium and help keep us operating.

All of the articles that were at our previous website will eventually be available here, but, regrettably, not all at the same URLs. So, if you are looking for an older article, please try again in the near future, when we have restored the historical content and implemented a search feature.

The design and programming of the website was done by board member Lee Phillips and the Alogus Research Corporation, which also donates web hosting. It is based partly on work done for the previous site by Tom Wanat and other members of the board.

The entire toolchain used to create and deliver this site consists of open source software. To mention just a key few of these tools, development is on a Ubuntu Linux laptop using Vim, Gimp, Imagemagick, Git, Rsync, Python, Clojure, and the Gnu userland; deployment is on Debian Linux with Apache, PostgreSQL, and Django.
