Donate to the Dave Brown Scholarship!

The Friends of Arlington's David M. Brown Planetarium partnered with the Arlington Community Foundation in 2012 to establish the David M. Brown Memorial Scholarship. Awarded each year to an Arlington high school student who plans to major in science, technology, engineering, or mathematics in college, this $8,000 scholarship honors the remarkable career and service of Arlington native son, David M. Brown - Navy pilot, medical doctor, gymnast, film maker, and NASA astronaut.

The goal is to raise $8,000 by December 31st. Please join us in reaching our goal, and then attend the presentation ceremony and reception next spring at the David M. Brown Planetarium.
