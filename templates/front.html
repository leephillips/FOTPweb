This weekend: Arlington Public Schools programming.

Friends of the Planetarium October Weekend: Commercial Space Flight and a Specially Commissioned Artwork

On October 14–16 the Planetarium will be host to a special program devoted to full-dome children’s shows, a special guest lecture about the commercial space program, and the world premiere of a work of video art designed specially for our facility.

We are proud to present an exclusive, specially commissioned full dome video, In This Convex Hull, made exclusively for the David M. Brown Planetarium by artist Brandon Morse. Thanks to your generous donations to the Morse Video fund, and our partnership with the Arlington Cultural Affairs Department, we get to share this new artwork with you FREE of charge! We will have several free showings of the piece each night. Just go here for reservations, and a description of the artist’s concept.
